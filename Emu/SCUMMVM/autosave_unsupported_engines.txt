Engines that do not support auto-save on exit (SIGUSR1 save-and-quit)
=====================================================================

These engines will still quit cleanly but will NOT save when exiting
via GameSwitcher or home button. This is no worse than a normal exit.

Autosave fully disabled:
- BladeRunner (has its own save system per Act)
- SCI (King's Quest, Space Quest, Leisure Suit Larry, etc.)
- BBVS (Beavis and Butt-Head in Virtual Stupidity)
- Petka
- AGS (some individual games opt out)

Autosave blocked by engine:
- Hypno (Spider: The Video Game)
- Freescape (Driller, Castle Master, Dark Side, Total Eclipse)
- Private (Private Eye)

Effectively blocked:
- SCUMM HE games (Pajama Sam, Putt-Putt, Freddi Fish, SPY Fox)
